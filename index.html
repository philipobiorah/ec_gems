EC GEMS LIMITED

Nigerian based company focused on mining and selling of natural rough quality gem stones.
We have been engaged actively in the mining sector since 2017. We finance, operate and maintain our mining sites. We get our gemstones directly from the source and we are not a third-party distributor. We pride ourselves in the mining of various top colour quality gemstones. Contact Us

EC GEMS LIMITED

We are registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and issued with the Certificate of Incorporation. Our registration number is RC 1776489. We have Small Scale Mining Lease (SSML) issued by the Mining Cadastre Office of the Federal Ministry of Mines and Steels Development of Nigeria. The Nigerian Export Promotion Council (NEPC) issued us with the Exporters’ Registration Certificate which certifies us to export our products. We receive payments from our buyers and customers through our online Payoneer platform and also through our financial institution -Zenith Bank Plc.

OUR PRODUCTS

We engage in the mining of top colour quality gemstones. We finance, operate and maintain our mining sites. Our mining activities are: Drilling, Gemstone Exploration, Mining logistics. We market our products on several offline and online trading platforms to individuals, private and corporate organizations.

Our products are primarily top colour quality natural gemstones which ranges from the variety of successes we register from our different mining sites such as – emerald, ruby, tourmaline, sapphire, morganite, etc. We deal on variety of rough natural gemstones and also polished gemstones.
